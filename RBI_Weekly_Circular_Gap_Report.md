**Two clear regulatory themes dominate this week:**
1. **Electronic Banking Transaction (EBT) liability framework** — 4 nearly-identical circulars (RBI/167–170) rolling out new definitions (EBT, Fraudulent EBT, Card Present/Not Present, customer/bank negligence) across Commercial Banks, SFBs, Payments Banks, and LABs. Effective **January 1, 2027**.
2. **Net Open Position (NOP) / FX risk capital methodology overhaul** — 9 circulars (RBI/155–162) revising how NOP and FX capital charge are computed, across Commercial Banks, SFBs, RRBs, UCBs, RCBs, LABs, AIFIs, and SPDs. Effective **April 1, 2027**.

A third, smaller cluster (RBI/146–149) grants a temporary **CRR/SLR exemption for long-tenor NRE deposits** through September 30, 2026, across RRBs, UCBs, RCBs, and SFBs.
